## Drop Rate Summary

For loot table implementation:

```
Rarity roll (per equipment drop):
01-50 → Common
51-80 → Uncommon
81-95 → Rare
96-99 → Legendary
00    → Cursed (1% = roll of 100 on d100, or 1/100)
```

### Slot Distribution (when equipment drops)

| Slot | Weight | Notes |
|------|:------:|-------|
| Weapon | 40% | Most common; 4 subtypes split by class |
| Armor | 25% | 3 weight classes by class |
| Hat | 20% | Universal; always usable |
| Boots | 15% | Universal; always usable |

### Weapon Subtype Distribution (within weapon drops)

| Subtype | Weight | Eligible |
|---------|:------:|----------|
| 1H Weapons | 40% | Warrior, Rogue |
| 2H Weapons | 20% | Warrior only |
| Magic Weapons | 20% | Mage only |
| Blunt Weapons | 20% | Cleric only |

When a weapon drops, roll subtype first, then rarity. If the dropped weapon's class restriction excludes all living party members, re-roll the subtype (not the rarity — the rarity is the dungeon's reward tier).
